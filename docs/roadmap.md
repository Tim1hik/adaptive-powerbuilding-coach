# Roadmap

## MVP

- Web-only installable PWA.
- Supabase Auth and PostgreSQL.
- Training diary, progress tracking, adaptive nutrition, readiness checks, Arena leaderboard.
- Built-in PPL x UL default training program.

## Later

- Native iOS and Android apps.
- Apple Health integration.
- Expanded program library and coaching intelligence.
- Mobile app funding milestone: `$100` to begin development and cover Apple Developer Program-related or publishing costs.
- Planned mobile benefits include Apple Health integration, activity and sleep automation, push reminders, offline workout diary, faster logging, deeper adaptation, and future premium analytics.
