# Training Adaptation

Readiness check-ins collect sleep quality, sleep duration, energy, stress, motivation, soreness, pain area, pain severity, and desire to train heavy.

Readiness score drives the first adaptation:

- 85-100: keep plan, optional heavy top set allowed.
- 70-84: keep plan, no PR suggestion.
- 50-69: reduce load 5-10% or remove 1-2 sets.
- Below 50: light technique session.

Pain overrides protect affected areas:

- Strong pain blocks heavy sets.
- Shoulder pain reduces or replaces heavy pressing.
- Low back pain reduces or replaces squat and deadlift patterns.
- Knee pain reduces squat intensity or substitutes the movement.
